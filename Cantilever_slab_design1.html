Answer 1

Assumptions

Parameter Value
Overall depth at fixed end D = 200 mm
Overall depth at free end D = 100 mm
Grade of Concrete M40, fck = 40 N/mm²
Grade of Steel Fe500, fy = 500 N/mm²
Clear cover d' = 20 mm (mild exposure)
Diameter of main steel φmain = 12 mm
Diameter of distribution steel φdistribution = 10 mm

Step 1: Calculate Effective Depth

d = D - φ/2 - d' = 200 - 12/2 - 20 = 174 mm

Step 2: Calculate Effective Span (Clause 22.2(c))

leff = Clear span + d/2 = 1.08 + 0.174/2 = 1.167 m
(1 - 4.6Mu/fckbd2) √(1 - 4.6Mu/fckbd2)

Step 3: Calculation of Design Load

Self weight of the slab = (0.2 + 0.1)/2 × 25 = 3.75 kN/m²

Factored dead load, WUDL = 1.5 × 3.75 = 5.625 kN/m²
Factored live load (UVL):
At free end: 1.5 × 107.5 = 161.25 kN/m²
At fixed end: 1.5 × 69.7 = 104.55 kN/m²

Step 4: Calculation of Moment and Shear Force

Bending Moment, Mu:

Mu = WUDL × l²/2 + (WuLL1 × l²/2) + (1/2 × l × WuLL × 2/3 × l)
Mu = 5.625 × (1.167)²/2 + (104.55 × 1.167²/2) + (1/2 × 1.167 × 56.7 × 2/3 × 1.167)
Mu = 107.62 ≈ 108 kNm

Shear Force, Vu:

Vu = (WuDL × l) + ((WuLL1 + WuLL2)/2) × l
Vu = (5.625 × 1.167) + ((161.25 + 104.55)/2) × 1.167 = 161.658 kN
